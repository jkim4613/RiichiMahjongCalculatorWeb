What is riichi mahjong?

Riichi mahjong is the Japanese version of the original game of mahjong that originated from China. It is a tabletop game that can be played by 3 or 4 players, where each player compete against each other to complete a hand and take points from the other players. The score is calculated based on the conditions the completed hand fulfills, as well as other factors
