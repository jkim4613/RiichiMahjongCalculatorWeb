Score Calculator

Below is a simplified score calculator you can use to get a general understanding of how much points you would earn from completing a hand with a combination of given conditions. You are also awarded and required to pay different amount of points based on whether you are the parent(equivalent to dealer) or the child. Note that some conditions cannot be fulfilled at the same time. Yakuman are special hands that override every other conditions. Once a yakuman is selected, the score will not change until unselected.
